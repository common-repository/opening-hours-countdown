A plugin that countdown the hours until your shop/restaurant etc. opening and closing. 


== Description ==

The plugin shows how many hours and minutes there are left until your store/restaurant etc. are opening or closing. In settings you can put in your own opening hours and also your timezone. 

To see how the plugin look like, please visit http://openinghoursplugin.com/.


== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin and activate it. 
2. Either use the plugin as a widget or use the shortcode [store_countdown] on either a page or a post.
